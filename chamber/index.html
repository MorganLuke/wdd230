School Carnival Brings Community Fun

The annual Foothill Elementary School summer kick-off carnival, held Friday May 20th, was a huge step towards feeling normal again. It has been 3 years since the largest carnival in our community has been able to proceed, and the turnout was nothing short of amazing. Months of hard work, countless fliers and sponsorship and donation requests, endless hours of planning and prepping led to an extremely successful carnival fundraiser for the FES PFA(Parent Faculty Association). This fundraiser is the largest fundraiser for the organization, which supports the students and programs at Foothill. Each grade level and special(Art, PE, Music, etc.) was in charge of supporting on of the carnival booths: teachers, parents, faculty, students and friends, all working side-by-side to create a memory for each and every child there. Silent auction donationswere also collected per grade level, each with a different theme.
