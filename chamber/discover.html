Discover Fun Things In And Around Auberry

Ski at China Peak

Have fun skiing China Peak with family and friends that features six chairlifts, three moving carpets, a tube hill and 1700 feet of terrain elevation change. They also have multiple skill level parks with boxes, rails, and jumps. They offer lodgind, RV parking and dining at the lodge. When the snow is gone they offer mountain bike trails accessable with the chairlifts. Its fun for the whole family.

Sierra High School

Visit a school rich woith history and culture. There are 300 students at the high school and 2200 in the district. Founded in 1921 it is nestled in a foothill vally overlooking the mountains. The school has strong ties to the local native american tribe and the school mascot is a chieftain. Drop in and take a tour of the campus, stay for a sporting event or visit the animals at the school farm. In no time you will feel apart of this tight knitted community.

Visit Shaver And Surrounding Lakes

Shaver Lake is a popular recreational lake surrounded by thick pine forests of the Sierra National Forest. There is a public boat launch ramp, a marina with boat rentals, camping, fishing and off-road 4x4 trails. The cute town of Shaver offers restaurants, lodging, shops and bars. There is something fun for everyone at Shaver Lake.

Auberry Small Businesses

Small Busnesses thrive in Auberry. There are Shops, restaurants, tradesmen, grocerystores and fuel. When you come to visit you'll have everything you need to explore Auberry and the surrounding mountains without having to drive back to the city of Fresno.

Tour A Home For Sale

Homes in Auberry range from $350,000 to $3,000,000. Homes are located on land ranging from two to 200 acres. These is a diverse mix of homes and property that make Auberry a rich community culture. Contact one our many qualified local real estate agents and tour some properties. You may find that you want to stay long term.
