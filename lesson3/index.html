Purpose

The Auberry Chamber of Commerce is the most prestigious chamber of commerce in the California Sierra Nevadas. Our members enjoy the benefits of a thriving community that actively participates in local businesses. The community offers a safe, clean, and affordable place for you to relocate your family or business. The Auberry chamber of commerce is here to support our members tap into new opportunities to grow their businesses. With over 100 local small businesses we help advocate for our members on issues that impact the economic stability of the community. We serve the community by partnering with charity organizations to give back to the community.
